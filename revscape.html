We arrived in Santa Maria and set up a table overlooking the fields. Then, with four hands, we began to play. The clouds rose and wrapped around us. Eyes closed, we kept playing, lost in the sound. When we finally opened our eyes and lifted our hands from the keyboard, the clouds were gone
